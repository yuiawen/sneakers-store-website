How can I place an order on your website?

Ordering is simple! Just browse our collection, select the sneakers you love, choose your size, and click on the "Add to Cart" button. Once you're done shopping, proceed to the checkout page, where you can review your order and complete the purchase.


What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Paypal) and secure online payment gateways. You can choose the payment option that suits you best during the checkout process.


How long does shipping take, and what are the costs?

Shipping times and costs may vary depending on your location and the shipping method you select. We offer standard and expedited shipping options, and the estimated delivery timeframes and costs will be displayed at checkout.


Can I track my order?

Absolutely! Once your order has been processed and shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your delivery.


What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If, for any reason, you are not happy with your sneakers, you may return them within 30 days of delivery. Please review our Returns and Exchanges policy for detailed instructions on how to initiate a return.


Are the sneakers on your website authentic?

Yes, we guarantee the authenticity of all the sneakers we sell. We source our products directly from reputable brands and authorized distributors, ensuring that you receive genuine merchandise.


Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to many countries. During the checkout process, you can enter your shipping address to see if we ship to your location. Please note that additional customs duties or taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations.


How can I contact your customer support team?

If you have any questions, concerns, or need assistance, our customer support team is here to help. You can reach us by phone or email us at unifoots@gmail.com. We strive to provide timely and helpful responses to all inquiries.


Do you offer discounts or promotions?

We frequently run special promotions and offer discounts on selected products. To stay updated on our latest deals and offers, sign up for our newsletter or follow us on social media. Don't miss out on exclusive discounts and exciting promotions!


Can I cancel or modify my order?

If you need to cancel or modify your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request, depending on the status of your order and the shipping process.


We hope these FAQs have provided you with helpful information. Should you have any additional queries or require further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our friendly customer support team. We value your satisfaction and look forward to serving you at our Sneakers Store. Happy shopping!
